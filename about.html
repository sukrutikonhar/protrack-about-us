Our story

Protrack is an ode to sportsmanship. Inspire by a passion to create a better environment fofr the spirit of sportsmanship, this high-performance academy focuses on enhancing the strength of athletes by enabling them to find their best selves. We empower young athletes with the best of guidance , trainers, and facilitates to further their dream of pursuing sports. Our ecosystem is equipped with the best manpower nd equipment to generate more confident and capable athletes in the coming years.

  • Our calendar is a potent and lively mix of routines centered around basic athletic movements, bodyweight excercises, resistence training, hill and sand training, and team sports activities.
